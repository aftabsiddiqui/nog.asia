Asia Network Operator Groups Hub: Connecting Communities

Welcome to nog.asia, your crowd sourced central hub for connecting with the vibrant community of Network Operator Groups across Asia. NOGs have always played an important role in Asia whether it was SANOG (South Asia Network Operators Group) or PacNOG (Pacific Network Operators Group) they all have played their role in enhancing internet infrastructure and promoting best practices among operators within the region. 

With each day, National NOGs are growing stronger and increasingly influential in the region. Currently, there are over 22 active national NOGs, highlighting the growth and reach of these communities. It is important to raise awareness about their presence so that those interested can connect with and contribute to these dynamic groups. 

On this page, you can explore a comprehensive list of NOGs, complete with direct links to their official websites. Each NOG entry on our site includes detailed information about their website, availability of mailing lists, instructions on how to subscribe to these lists, and updates on upcoming events. 

This layout is designed to provide quick and straightforward access to the resources you need. Whether you are new to NOGs or a seasoned professional looking to engage more deeply with your peers.
